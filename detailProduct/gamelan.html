GAMELAN Gamelan is a traditional Indonesian musical ensemble consisting of various instruments such as gongs, kenong, saron, bonang, and kendang. Gamelan is typically played in groups, producing distinctive harmonies and rich melodies. This ensemble is commonly found in different regions of Indonesia, especially in Java, Bali, and Sunda. It is often used in traditional ceremonies, dances, wayang performances (shadow puppetry), and other cultural events, reflecting the deep cultural heritage of Indonesia.
